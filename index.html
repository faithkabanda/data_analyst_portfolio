Project 1
Query Output Report Generation

Project Summary: A company has various clients who are required to provide regular updates of their actions, setbacks and progress in the form of income reports, account details, expense reports, loans taken reports as well as loans given reports. This projects extracts all raw data reports of all participating firms in 3 different cities in Zimbabwe: Bulawayo, Gweru and Mutare. The objectives are to utilize Python and MS Excel to investigate rates at which information is provided, its validity and identify firms that have queries that need to be resolved. Data cleaning, wrangling and consolidation is done in Python whilst exploratory data analysis and output report generation are completed in MS Excel.
